⚙ Calorie-burn settings
Resting calories use Mifflin–St Jeor BMR + NEAT (non-exercise activity thermogenesis — walking, stairs, errands), prorated to the current time of day (male formula). BMR and NEAT are stored per day, so historical values don't shift when you change a setting. The default NEAT above applies to new days; click the ✎ next to "NEAT" in the burn card to adjust a specific day. Active calories come from manual entries and from Strava workouts Claude pulls in via chat.
Data is saved as plain Markdown files in your local Health Tracker folder · food-log/YYYY-MM-DD.md · food-log/_library.md · symptom-log.md. Resting burn uses the Mifflin–St Jeor BMR formula.
